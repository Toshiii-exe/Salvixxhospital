Our Services

General Medicine

Comprehensive healthcare for all ages, focusing on disease prevention and health promotion.

Surgery

Advanced surgical procedures performed by our skilled surgeons using state-of-the-art technology.

Maternity Care

Supportive and personalized care for expecting mothers from prenatal to postpartum stages.

Pediatrics

Specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents in a family-friendly environment.

Oncology

Comprehensive cancer care, including diagnosis, treatment, and supportive services.

Cardiology

Expert care for heart conditions, including prevention, diagnosis, and treatment.

Urology

Treatment of urinary tract conditions and male reproductive health issues, including kidney stones and bladder problems.

Nephrology

Management and treatment of kidney-related diseases, including chronic kidney disease and dialysis services.

Dermatology

Diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions, including rashes, acne, and skin cancers.

Radiology and Imaging

Diagnostic imaging services, including X-rays, MRI, CT scans, and ultrasounds, for accurate diagnosis.

Nutrition and Dietetics

Dietary assessment and nutritional guidance for patients with specific health conditions or those requiring specialized diets.

Podiatry

Treatment of foot, ankle, and lower extremity conditions, including bunions, fractures, and diabetic foot care.

Geriatrics

Specialized care for older adults, focusing on managing chronic diseases, mobility issues, and overall elderly health.

Psychiatry and Mental Health

Comprehensive care for mental health conditions, including depression, anxiety, and addiction.

Anesthesiology and Pain Management

Pain relief services during surgeries and other medical procedures, and management of chronic pain conditions.
