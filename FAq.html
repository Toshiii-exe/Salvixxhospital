Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

Our hospital is open 24/7, including holidays.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment online through our reservation system or by calling our main line.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance providers. Please contact us for more details.

Where are you located?

We are located at 123 Health Avenue, Wellness City, near the central park.

What should I bring for my appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, your insurance card, any referral documents, and a list of your current medications. For specific departments, additional documentation may be required, so please check with us ahead of time.

Can I visit patients in the hospital?

Yes, visitors are welcome during visiting hours. Please check our visiting policy on our website or call the hospital for any specific restrictions or requirements related to patient visits.

Are there services available for non-English speakers?

Yes, we offer translation and interpretation services to assist non-English speaking patients. Please inform us ahead of your appointment so we can make the necessary arrangements.

Is there parking available at the hospital?

Yes, we have ample parking available for patients and visitors. There are designated areas for patient drop-off, accessible parking, and visitor parking. Valet services are also available.

Do you offer telemedicine services?

Yes, we offer telemedicine services for certain types of consultations and follow-up appointments. Please check with your provider or our scheduling department to see if telemedicine is available for your needs.
